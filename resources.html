Watch Handover Checklist

  • main engine and propulsion status;
  • generators on load and standby;
  • fuel, lubricating oil, cooling water, air, bilge, and ballast status;
  • alarms, bypasses, defects, leaks, and abnormal trends;
  • ongoing maintenance and permits to work;
  • bridge orders, chief engineer’s orders, and expected operations.

Emergency Initial Report Formula

Type of emergency + exact location + present severity + operational effect + action in progress + assistance required.
