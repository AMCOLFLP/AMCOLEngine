Listening & Pronunciation Lab

1. Numbers and Measurements

Practise: 0.8 bar, 4.5 bar, 78 °C, 440 V, 60 Hz, 1,250 rpm, 45 m³/h, 0215 hours.

2. Similar-Sounding Technical Terms

ContrastPronunciation focus
suction / sectionKeep the first vowel and final consonants clear.
bearing / clearingStress the first syllable in bearing.
pressure / pleasureUse the /ʃ/ sound in pressure.
valve / valueFinish valve with a clear /v/ sound.
leak / lickHold the long /iː/ sound in leak.
trip / dripMake the first consonant clear.

3. Alarm Listening

Listen and write: equipment identity, alarm parameter, current reading, trend, and required action.

4. Shadowing Drill

Repeat immediately after the model: “Generator number three synchronized, breaker closed, automatic load sharing selected, current stable, no alarms active.”
