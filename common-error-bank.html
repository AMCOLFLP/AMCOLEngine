Common Error Bank

High-Frequency Errors

Common errorCorrect formReason
The pump is damage.The pump is damaged. / The pump has been damaged.Use adjective/passive form.
Pressure are low.Pressure is low.Pressure is uncountable singular.
We have changed it yesterday.We changed it yesterday.Use past simple with a finished time.
Must to stop the motor.You must stop the motor.Modal + base verb.
Open the valve complete.Open the valve fully.Use an adverb for the action.
The alarm is happened.The alarm has activated. / The alarm occurred.Choose an active event verb.
The oil is leak.Oil is leaking. / There is an oil leak.Use verb + ing or noun form.
I think bearing broken.A knocking sound is coming from the bearing area.Report observation before diagnosis.
Okay.Understood. I will start pump No. 2 now.Repeat critical instruction.
Finished.Pump started; discharge pressure 3.5 bar and stable.Report result, not only completion.

Correction Routine

  1. Identify the equipment and operation.
  2. Decide whether the message is an instruction, current status, completed event, or recommendation.
  3. Select the correct tense or modal.
  4. Add number, unit, location, and direction.
  5. Read the final sentence aloud for clarity.
