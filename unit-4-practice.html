Performance Task

In pairs, complete a two-minute role-play based on the unit scenario. Your exchange must include:

  • exact equipment identity and location;
  • at least one reading, position, symptom, or hazard;
  • one clear instruction and one accurate read-back;
  • a completion report with the result;
  • one short written logbook or maintenance entry.

Student Self-Assessment Checklist

Unit Exit Ticket

  1. Write three key terms from this unit and define them.
  2. Write one normal-status report and one abnormal-status report.
  3. Write the exact sentence you would use to confirm that an assigned action is completed.
